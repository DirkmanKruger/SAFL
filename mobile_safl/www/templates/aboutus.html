Our History

Started in 2015, The South african Fishing Log Application came about through the culmination of various campfire stories about The One that got away.

As a couple of fishing buddies, with fortunately a few developers between us, we got to thinking and eventually hashed out a plan of how we would better facilitate our fishing expeditions. At first we would share our catches with a social media group we formed, but this didn't give us the opportunity of actually keeping track of what was caught, when where or how the catches were made.

So after a couple of design meetings at our local pub, the initial design of our Fishing Log App was born and we haven't looked back since.
